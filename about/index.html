About Me

I’m interested in anything interesting, but unfortunately, or maybe fortunately, we happen to have limited amount of time on Earth. So, I try to focus on a small (perhaps negligible) subset, which is bioinformatics: in particular phylogenetics, metagenomics, and some sequence analysis.

Currently, I’m a graduate student in the Bioinformatics and Systems Biology PhD program at the University of California, San Diego, under the mentorship of Siavash Mirarab.
