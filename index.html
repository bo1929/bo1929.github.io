KRANK has been accepted in RECOMB 2024

Our paper titled “Memory-bound k-mer selection for large evolutionary diverse reference libraries” has been accepted to RECOMB 2024. Hopefully, I’ll be in Cambridge (April 29 - May 2, 2024).

Our paper has been published in Bioinformatics

We published a paper in Bioinformatics. In this paper, we developed a new approach for community detection in multi-subject and dynamic networks, in particular networks constructed from scRNA-seq data.

Our paper has been published in Natural Language Engineering

We just published a paper in Natural Language Engineering after a long revision process. This work was a continuation and an extension of my undergraduate graduation project.

CONSULT-II won the best paper award

I presented at RECOMB Comparative Genomics 2023, in Istanbul (see my slides). Our paper, with Elenora Rachtman and Siavash Mirarab, CONSULT-II: Taxonomic Identification Using Locality Sensitive Hashing won the best paper award.

I successfully defended my MSc thesis

I have finished my thesis, titled “Automated behavioral analysis of asleep fruit fly” – which is now available online, and obtained my MSc degree. The defense was on 26 July 2022, you can find my slides here. Jury members are listed below, I do appreciate their time and invaluable comments. Öznur Taştan, Sabancı Üniversitesi (advisor) Sündüz Keleş, University of Wisconsin-Madison (advisor) Onur Varol, Sabancı Üniversitesi Gözde Akar, Orta Doğu Teknik Üniversitesi Ercüment Çiçek, Bilkent Üniversitesi

I presented a poster and a spotlight presentation at MLCB 2021

I attended to 16th Machine Learning for Computational Biology Conference (MLCB), and presented our work in progress, titled “MuDCoD: Multi-Subject Community Detection in Dynamic Gene Networks” (see my slides, note that I ended up using a simplified version due to the time constraint). The 2-page long abstract is not available online, but hopefully a preprint will be soon. We also had a poster presentation on the second day.
